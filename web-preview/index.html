How it works

Try the full flow — browse, filter, tap meals, add to cart, and place an order. All interactive.

1
Browse & filter meals
Use the filter chips on the phone to filter by cuisine or diet. Tap any meal card to see full details, the cook's story, and batch availability.
2
Add to cart
Tap "Add to Cart" from the detail page. Notice the live batch count goes down. Cart icon updates with a badge count. Multiple items, multiple cooks.
3
Checkout & track
See itemized bill with platform fee + delivery. Place order — it appears live in the Orders tab with real-time status simulation.
+
Feed a Neighbour 🤲
On any meal detail page, tap "Feed a Neighbour" to anonymously donate a meal. Unique to Maase — community-first ordering.
